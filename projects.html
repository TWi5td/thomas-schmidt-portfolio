Modbus/DNP Simulation Platform (Capstone, Spring 2025)

Transistor Characterization (VLSI Design, Spring 2025)

Analyzed NMOS transistor behavior in Cadence, plotting ID(VDS) and ID(VGS) curves to identify cut-off, linear, and saturation regions. Documented threshold voltages and subthreshold slopes. Tools: Cadence, Multisim.
